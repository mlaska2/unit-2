Where's the Air Pollution Coming From?

CO2 Emissions by State from 1990 - 2017

Carbon Dioxide (CO2) emissions stem from various sources, including the burning of fossil fuels and deforestation. In 2017, CO2 emissions in the United States accounted for 82% of total greenhouse gas emissions. 1 Here, you can explore the CO2 emissions from each state over a 28 year period.
